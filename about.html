About Me

I’m Sagdah Elshamy, recognized as one of the youngest professional makeup artists and special effects specialists in Egypt.

With over six years of experience in the beauty industry, I have had the privilege of collaborating with prestigious international magazines and have worked with more than 150 models in a single year.

Known for my distinctive special effects fantasy creations and innovative editorial looks, I bring a unique artistic vision to the fashion industry.

My work seamlessly blends creativity, precision, and a deep understanding of beauty trends, making me a sought-after artist in both editorial and fantasy makeup.

Whether it’s crafting ethereal fantasy aesthetics or designing avant-garde looks for high-fashion editorials, I am dedicated to pushing boundaries and delivering exceptional artistry that leaves a lasting impression.
